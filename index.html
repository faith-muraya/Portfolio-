Welcome to the dynamic portfolio of the Group 5 students from Moringa School, a leading institution renowned for its excellence in tech education. Our group is a close-knit cohort of aspiring computer scientists, each bringing a unique set of skills and aspirations to the table. Together, we form a diverse team that is united by a shared passion for exploring the exciting realm of computer science. Meet the team:

Jackline, Faith, Linder, Eric, and Ezra.

Jackline

Hello, I'm Jackline, a passionate software developer with a knack for turning complex ideas into elegant, functional solutions. I thrive on challenges, continuously expanding my skill set to craft innovative and efficient applications. I'm committed to creating software that not only meets client needs but also pushes the boundaries of what's possible. Let's build the future one code at a time.

Faith

Hello! I'm Faith, a frontend web developer dedicated to crafting beautiful and user-friendly digital experiences.
